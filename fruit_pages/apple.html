Healthy Apple Juice and Smoothie Recipes

Apple Carrot Ginger Juice

This refreshing juice combines the sweetness of apples with the goodness of carrots and a kick of ginger.

Ingredients:

  • 2 apples
  • 3 medium carrots
  • 1-inch piece of fresh ginger

Green Apple Kale Smoothie

This green smoothie is packed with nutrients from kale, spinach, and green apples.

Ingredients:

  • 1 green apple
  • 1 cup kale leaves
  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey

Apple Berry Smoothie

This berrylicious smoothie combines the sweetness of apples with the tartness of mixed berries.

Ingredients:

  • 2 apples
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup water or apple juice
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
