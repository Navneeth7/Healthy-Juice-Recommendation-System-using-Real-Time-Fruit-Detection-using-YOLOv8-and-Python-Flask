Healthy Orange Juice and Smoothie Recipes

Orange Carrot Turmeric Juice

This vibrant juice combines the tanginess of oranges with the goodness of carrots and a hint of turmeric.

Ingredients:

  • 3 oranges
  • 2 medium carrots
  • 1/2 inch piece of fresh turmeric

Orange Mango Banana Smoothie

This tropical smoothie is bursting with flavor from oranges, sweet mangoes, and creamy bananas.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oranges
  • 1 ripe mango
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup orange juice

Orange Berry Smoothie

This delightful smoothie combines the zesty flavor of oranges with the sweetness of mixed berries.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oranges
  • 1 cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup coconut milk
  • 1 tablespoon honey
